The Conception

The Gymkhana club at Chiang Mai in northern Thailand was conceived in 1898 by a handful of expatriate gentlemen with the object of using the club as a means of encouraging sport in Northern Siam. For the club to achieve this goal it should be managed so as to meet the needs of the members and their guests and be run as a profitable enterprise where all profits are circulated back into the club for continual improvement.

The club would also act as a focal point where members could meet up during their work breaks and together with their wives enjoy a haven of tranquility, engage in conversation, participate in various sports and relax on the veranda whilst the heat of the afternoon sun gave way to cooling breezes from the adjacent mountains.

The British playwright and novelist, Somerset Maugham, wrote when he visited the Gymkhana club while touring through the Chiang Mai district in the 1920s: "Although horse racing is no more, there are excellent facilities for golf, tennis, squash and cricket. The Gymkhana club is well worth a visit and provides a chance to take a step back into a different kind of Thai history."

His observation still applies to this day, nearly a century and a quarter later.

Championship Golf Course

The Chiang Mai Gymkhana Club offers a 9-hole parkland golf course designed to challenge players of all skill levels. Set among mature trees and lush tropical vegetation, the course provides a spectacular backdrop while maintaining the challenge and playability expected by serious golfers.

Our golf facilities include a well-stocked pro shop, driving range with 10 practice positions, and professional instruction available. Whether you are a beginner seeking to learn the game or an experienced golfer looking for a friendly competition, our course and facilities will exceed your expectations.

Green fees include caddy services, and members enjoy substantial discounts. Our golf pro shop offers clothing, equipment, and carefully curated selections of pre-owned clubs at excellent member pricing.

Tennis Facilities

Our tennis courts meet professional standards and welcome players of all abilities. The club maintains several courts in excellent condition, offering members and guests the opportunity to enjoy this classic sport year-round in Thailand's pleasant climate.

Regular tennis coaching is available for members seeking to improve their game, and the club hosts friendly matches and tournaments throughout the year. Whether you prefer casual play or competitive tennis, our facilities and community provide an ideal environment.

Chiang Mai Tennis Club

Cricket at Gymkhana

Cricket holds a special place in the history and traditions of the Chiang Mai Gymkhana Club. Our grounds host regular club matches, tournaments, and have earned recognition as one of Thailand's premier cricket venues.

The cricket community at Gymkhana spans all experience levels, from those learning the basics to experienced players representing the club in regional competitions. Our facilities and active membership make this an ideal place to enjoy cricket in a welcoming, competitive atmosphere.

Restaurant & Bar

The Gymkhana Club restaurant offers a welcoming atmosphere for members and their guests, featuring a thoughtful menu that blends local Thai cuisine with international favorites. Our skilled kitchen team prepares fresh, flavorful dishes using quality ingredients.

The bar provides a relaxed setting for socializing before or after sporting activities, and our event spaces are ideal for celebrations, private dinners, and special occasions. The restaurant and bar embody the club's long tradition of hospitality and community gathering.
