Restaurant & Bar

Welcoming dining and social spaces for members and guests

The Restaurant

The Chiang Mai Gymkhana Club restaurant provides a welcoming atmosphere where members and their guests enjoy excellent food, friendly service, and a relaxed social environment. Our kitchen team prepares fresh, flavorful dishes that blend authentic Thai cuisine with international favorites, accommodating diverse tastes and preferences.

The restaurant menu reflects careful sourcing of quality ingredients and thoughtful preparation. Whether you are seeking traditional Thai flavors or classic international cuisine, our culinary team creates dishes that will satisfy. Regular seasonal specials and featured items ensure that frequent diners always find something new to enjoy.

The dining room offers a comfortable setting that encourages both casual meals and lingering over dinner with friends. Outdoor seating is available when weather permits, providing pleasant dining among the club's tropical grounds.

Bar & Social Space

The club bar provides a relaxed setting for before and after sports activities, offering a full selection of beverages and light refreshments. Members and their guests enjoy this space for casual conversation, socializing, and connecting with the broader club community.

The bar and adjacent social areas create a welcoming environment for casual gatherings. Whether you are meeting friends after tennis, relaxing following a round of golf, or simply spending time in the club community, these spaces embody the social traditions that make Gymkhana special.

Private Events & Celebrations

The Gymkhana Club event spaces provide an elegant setting for special occasions and celebrations. Whether you are planning a private dinner, business gathering, or milestone celebration, our facilities and catering team create memorable occasions.

The club has hosted countless celebrations over its history, from intimate dinners to larger gatherings. Our staff are experienced in coordinating all details to ensure your event runs smoothly. Custom menu options are available to suit your preferences and guest requirements.

Romantic Celebrations & Weddings

The Gymkhana Club's grounds and spaces provide a beautiful setting for weddings, engagement parties, and other romantic celebrations. The combination of natural beauty, elegant facilities, and exceptional hospitality creates a memorable backdrop for life's most important moments.

Our catering team can work with you to create a customized menu and celebration plan that reflects your vision. From intimate family gatherings to larger wedding receptions, we have the space, experience, and dedicated staff to make your celebration special.

To discuss your specific event needs and explore options, please contact the club office to arrange a consultation with our events coordinator.
