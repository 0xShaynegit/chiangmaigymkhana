About Chiang Mai Gymkhana

Historic sports and social community in northern Thailand

Our Mission

The object of the Chiang Mai Gymkhana Club is the encouragement of sport and social community in northern Thailand. For the club to achieve this goal, it is managed to meet the needs of members and their guests, operated as a financially sustainable enterprise where all profits are reinvested in improving facilities and programs.

Over many decades, the club has developed a tradition of welcoming sport, friendship, and community. Members from diverse backgrounds share a passion for golf, tennis, cricket, and the social bonds that make a club more than just a venue for athletic activity.

Our Heritage

The Chiang Mai Gymkhana Club has a rich history as one of northern Thailand's premier sports and social institutions. The club's grounds have hosted generations of golfers, tennis players, cricketers, and members who came to enjoy sport and community in a welcoming setting.

The traditions established over the club's long history remain central to what we do today. Excellence in sports facilities and programming, warm hospitality, and a genuine sense of community are the foundations upon which Gymkhana stands.

World-Class Facilities

The Chiang Mai Gymkhana Club features championship-standard sports facilities set among natural tropical landscapes. Our 9-hole golf course, professional tennis courts, and premier cricket grounds reflect our commitment to providing the highest quality sporting experience.

The clubhouse provides comfortable gathering spaces, a restaurant and bar, and event facilities suitable for celebrations and private occasions. The combination of excellent sports facilities and social spaces creates an environment where members can enjoy both athletic activity and genuine community.

Membership Options

The Chiang Mai Gymkhana Club offers a range of membership options designed to accommodate different needs, playing frequencies, and preferences. Whether you are a serious golfer, a casual tennis player, or someone seeking the full club experience with dining and social activities, we have a membership package that will suit you.

Individual Membership

Full access to all club facilities including golf, tennis, cricket, dining, and social spaces. The primary membership option for regular club users.

Family Membership

Extended membership allowing spouse and dependent children to enjoy all club facilities. Perfect for families seeking year-round recreational activities.

Corporate Membership

Designed for businesses seeking membership benefits for employees and clients. Offers flexibility and value for companies of all sizes.

Limited Membership

Tailored membership options for those with more limited playing schedules. Includes access to selected facilities or specific sports activities.

Joining Gymkhana

Prospective members are encouraged to contact the club office to discuss membership options, visit the facilities, and learn more about our community. We welcome inquiries from individuals and organizations seeking to become part of the Gymkhana tradition.

The membership application process is straightforward and welcoming. Our staff can answer questions about different membership tiers, fee structures, and facility access. We invite you to experience firsthand why members have chosen to make Gymkhana part of their sporting and social life.
