The Course

A Round Worth Playing

The Chiang Mai Gymkhana Club golf course is a 9-hole parkland design that presents a fair challenge to golfers of all ability levels. Matured trees line the fairways, providing beauty and strategic challenge throughout the round. The course layout offers a wonderful balance between playability and championship-level competition.

Each hole is thoughtfully designed to take advantage of the natural landscape while maintaining the high standards expected by our membership. Whether playing your first round or your hundredth, you will find the course engaging, fair, and memorable. The elevation changes and tree-lined fairways create an experience that rivals courses in far more prominent golf destinations.

Our grounds crew maintains the course to the highest standards, ensuring that greens, fairways, and rough are always in excellent condition regardless of the season.

Golf has been played on this land since the club's founding in 1898, making the Gymkhana course the oldest in northern Thailand. The original layout was designed for simultaneous use with polo and cricket — which is why the routing is unlike any modern course you have played. The fairways were historically maintained by grazing cattle rather than machinery. The hazards include trees that have been growing here for over a century. Explore the course's history.

Practice Facilities

Driving Range

Our driving range provides 10 practice positions with quality turf and consistent lighting. Members can work on their game in a relaxed, professional environment with targets at varying distances. The range is ideal for warming up before your round or for focused practice sessions to refine your swing.

The practice facilities also include a short game area where you can work on chipping and pitching. Whether you are preparing for a tournament or simply maintaining your game, our range offers the space and quality you need to practice effectively.

Equipment & Gear

Pro Shop

Our pro shop is located conveniently at the green-fee office and offers a curated selection of golf equipment and apparel. We stock quality golf balls, comfortable clothing designed for tropical golf, stylish hats, and professional accessories to enhance your game.

Members enjoy substantial discounts on selected items, and we regularly feature specials with exceptional value. We also maintain a collection of carefully selected pre-owned golf clubs from quality manufacturers, offering excellent bargains for members seeking to expand or upgrade their bag.

Instruction

Professional Coaching

The club arranges professional golf instruction for members at all levels. Whether you are learning the fundamentals, working to break a specific score, or preparing for competition, our coaching professionals provide personalized instruction tailored to your goals.

Lessons are available individually or in small groups, and can be arranged through the club office. Professional coaching accelerates improvement and ensures that you are developing the proper fundamentals to enjoy golf for a lifetime.

Competition

Tournaments

The golf community at Chiang Mai Gymkhana hosts regular club tournaments, friendly matches, and social events throughout the year. These competitions provide opportunities to challenge yourself, connect with fellow golfers, and enjoy the camaraderie that makes club golf special.

From monthly medal competitions to annual club championships, there are numerous opportunities to play in an organised competitive setting. After your round, the restaurant veranda overlooking the grounds is the natural place to settle the match. Even if tournament golf is new to you, we welcome all skill levels and provide a supportive environment for friendly competition.

Pricing

Green Fees & Membership

Green fees include caddy services, which provide valuable local knowledge and assistance during your round. Our caddies are knowledgeable, professional, and appreciate gratuities for outstanding service. Member green fees are substantially discounted, offering excellent value for regular golfers.

For current fee information and specific pricing, please contact the club office at +66 53 241035 extension 12. Fees may vary seasonally, so we encourage you to check back regularly or call for the latest rates.

A full range of membership options is available to suit different playing frequencies and preferences. Whether you seek daily access, limited annual play, or corporate membership for your business, the club can structure a package to fit your needs. Members also enjoy access to the tennis courts and the full club facilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Chiang Mai Gymkhana the oldest golf course in northern Thailand?

Yes. Golf has been played at the Chiang Mai Gymkhana Club since the club's founding in 1898, making it the oldest golf course in northern Thailand. The 9-hole parkland layout was originally designed for simultaneous use with polo and cricket — a quirk of the grounds that still shapes the routing today. Read more unusual facts about the club.

What are the green fees for golf in Chiang Mai at Gymkhana?

Green fees are available for both members and visiting guests. Contact the club at +66-53-241-035 or visit the pro shop for current rates. The course is open to non-members and offers one of the most historic golfing experiences in Chiang Mai.

How many holes does the Gymkhana golf course have?

The Chiang Mai Gymkhana golf course is a 9-hole parkland course. Matured trees line the fairways and the layout offers a balance between playability and challenge. A driving range and pro shop are also on site.

Does Gymkhana offer golf coaching in Chiang Mai?

Yes. Professional golf coaching is available at the Chiang Mai Gymkhana Golf Club for players of all levels. The pro shop can arrange lessons and equipment hire. Contact the club for current coaching schedules and rates.
